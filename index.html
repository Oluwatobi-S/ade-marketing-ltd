What We Offer

At Ade Marketing Ltd, we provide a comprehensive range of services tailored to meet the unique needs of black-owned
businesses.


Our goal is to empower these businesses to thrive and achieve lasting success through innovative and effective marketing
strategies.





Here is a list of all the services that we offer:



  • Brand Development - We help you build a strong, recognizable brand that resonates with your target audience.
  • Digital Marketing - Our digital marketing services are designed to increase your online visibility and drive growth.
  • Social Media Management - We manage your social media presence to engage your audience and build a loyal community.
  • Content Creation - High-quality content is key to connecting with your audience.
  • Strategy and Consulting - We provide expert advice and strategic guidance to help you navigate the digital landscape.
  • Innovation and Trends - We aim to actively stay ahead of the curve by embracing the latest marketing trends and technologies.
  • Community and Collaboration - We believe in the power of community and collaboration.

Consultation Call

At Ade Marketing Ltd, our consultation calls are designed to provide you with a comprehensive overview of how our services can meet your specific marketing needs. During the 30-minute call, our experienced marketing consultants will discuss your business goals, target audience, and current marketing strategies.

We will evaluate your brand's strengths and identify areas for improvement, offering tailored advice and solutions to help you achieve your objectives. This consultation is a valuable opportunity to gain insights into the latest marketing trends and best practices, ensuring your business stays ahead of the competition. When you book a consultation call with us, you can expect a personalized and engaging conversation that focuses on your unique challenges and how Ade Marketing Ltd can support your growth.


What to Expect

Upon booking your consultation call, you will receive a confirmation email with all the necessary details, including the date, time, and a link to join the call. During our conversation, we will start by understanding your business's history and vision. Our consultants will then delve into your current marketing efforts,
analyzing what's working and what might need adjustment. Whether you're looking to boost your online presence, enhance your brand identity, or develop a robust marketing strategy, we are here to provide expert guidance tailored to your needs.

About Us

Ade Marketing Ltd is dedicated to empowering and supporting up-and-coming black businesses in the UK. We understand
the unique challenges that black entrepreneurs face, and we are committed to providing tailored marketing solutions that
help these businesses thrive and succeed.


Our team of experienced professionals offers a wide range of services, including brand development, digital marketing,
social media management, and content creation. We work closely with our clients to understand their goals and develop
strategies that deliver measurable results.


At Ade Marketing Ltd, we believe in the power of community and collaboration. Our mission is to elevate black-owned
businesses, helping them to reach their full potential and make a lasting impact in their industries.

Our History

Samuel Aderoyeje started his journey through his desire to see his peers acheive greatness. This desire led him to
encourage the people around him into their untapped potential.
He first started by advising his peers on social media platforms with techniques to further promote their project
and that then progressed to the entrepreneurs trusting Sam with their whole social media pages. Sam would then
create campaigns through innovative ideas, starting new trends and leading the marketing in whatever direction
he chooses

From the tender age of 17, Sam has been working with numerous musicians and various clothing brands with the
same goal in mind. Growth! Sam's passion to see what people can do when the opportunity arises has pushed him
to create Ade Marketing LTD as he continues to put his all into seeing the best out of all that he works with.
Providing new opportunities and breaking glass ceilings.

Since our inception, we have been on a steady growth trajectory. Driven by a passion for empowering black
businesses and a commitment to delivering exceptional results, we have built a solid reputation within the
community. Our initial focus was on building strong relationships and offering personalized consulting services to
help businesses navigate the digital landscape.
